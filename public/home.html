Welcome to Resettlement Hub

A nonprofit and volunteer networking hub centered on refugee resettlement efforts

There are plenty of other volunteering websites the world over, but this one was created by a guy lucky enough to have years of experience (some would say a lifetime) helping refugees resettle in the U.S. That said, I've designed the site to be admin-centric, but also for use by anybody interested in finding ways to help those fleeing from disasters.
The best I can hope for is that this site has helped you in your mission to help refugees and asylum seekers in any way. Thank you for visiting, and enjoy your stay!
